About Me

I've always been fascinated with and passionate about Information Technology, especially platform and infrastructure, and am excited about the explosive success of the Public Cloud and the advent of IoT, PaaS and Serverless Computing. I was fortunate to have spent much of my career working for Sun Microsystems so had a front row seat during the shift to networking computing, development of the WWW, Java and commercialization of the Internet. Those were exciting times for sure and layed groundwork for the mass disruption and accelerated innovation we're seeing today with DevOps, Containers and Hybrid Multi-Cloud technologies. Change is the only constant.

I'm CISSP Certified, AWS Solution Architect Associate Certified and am currently working on AWS Certified Developer. I still use vi(m) and these days work primarily in Python. I prefer OSX and Linux for development having been a BSD UNIX guy since my early days in the Purdue ECE lab. I enjoy hacking small single-board linux based computers (CHIP, Raspberry Pi) and integrating home automation services and devices.

For fun I also enjoy travel, running, yoga, as well as playing and promoting Segway Polo. Yes, that's really a thing.

About Me

I'm originally from the Midwest and moved to Northern CA after college. Currently living in N. Texas near Frisco. Over the years I've had the pleasure of working for large established corporations such as Amdahl, Sun Microsystems and Oracle as well as small start-ups like Rubrik in a variety of engineering, architecture and leadership roles.

I code primarily in Python 3 using Atom, Vim, Chrome and Visual Studio Code. Recently I've focused on full-stack development leveraging a server-less approach with AWS Lambda whenever possible. This keeps operational considerations, as well as costs, minimal. When I need a persistent datastore, I use DynamoDB or RDS.

I prefer Mac OSX and Linux for development and started programming in C on BSD UNIX while at Purdue ECE. My Windows experience is more limited to end-user and home system admin. I enjoy hacking small single-board Linux based computer (Raspberry Pi) based project and integrating home automation (Wink, LIFX, Nest, Sonos, etc.) with cloud services.

For fun I like running, skiing, motorcycles (currently a R1200RT), as well as playing and promoting Segway Polo. Yes, that's actually a thing. I'd love to get a local team organized in the N. Dallas area!

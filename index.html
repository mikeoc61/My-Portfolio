About Me

I've always been fascinated by Information Technology, especially platform and infrastructure, and am excited about the explosive success of the Public Cloud and the advent of IoT and Serverless Computing. I was fortunate to have spent the late 80's and 90's working for Sun Microsystems so got to experience the shift from minicomputer to networking computing, development of the WWW, Java and commercialization of the Internet. I also learned to appreciate the importance of good architecture.

I'm CISSP certified, AWS Solution Architect Associate Certified and am currently working on AWS Certified Developer. It's been many years since I was a full-time developer but think it's important as a IT professional to keep my hands dirty and have a grounded understanding of how things work. I still use VI(M) and am a fan of Python. I prefer Mac OS having been a UNIX guy since my early days in the Purdue ECE lab. I enjoy hacking small single-board linux based computers (CHIP, Raspberry PI) and integrating home automation devices using services such as Lambda, Yonomi and IFTTT. I'm currently using a mix of Ring, Wink, Lifx, Echo, Wemo, Sonos, AWS Button, GE and Lutron.

For fun I also enjoy travel, riding my R1200 RT and playing and promoting Segway Polo. Yes, that's really a thing.
